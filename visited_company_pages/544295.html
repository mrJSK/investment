Key Points

Business Overview:[1]
a) The Property Share Investment Trust (REIT) is India’s first registered Small and Medium Real Estate Investment Trust.
b) It plans to carry on the activity of a small and medium real estate investment trust through one or more Schemes
c) Each Scheme may have its own investment objectives
d) The investment of the Schemes shall only be in completed and revenue generating real estate properties through SPVs.

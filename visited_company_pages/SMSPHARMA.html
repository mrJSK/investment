About

SMS Pharmaceuticals Ltd is a diversified and integrated pharmaceutical company with interests in Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (API) and Intermediates.[1]

Key Points

Product Portfolio
It is an integrated pharmaceutical company specializing in Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (API) and complex Intermediates, under various therapeutic categories including Anti-ulcer, anti-fungal, anti-migraine, anti-viral, anti-diabetic, anti-retroviral, etc. with global leadership position in 10+ therapeutic products.

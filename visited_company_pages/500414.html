About

Timex Group India Limited is a group company of Tanager Group B.V. (formerly known as Timex Group B.V.). The Co is engaged in manufacturing and trading of watches and rendering of related after sales service. The Company also provides information and technology support services to the Group Companies. [1][2]

Key Points

History
Co entered India in 1988 as a joint venture with Jayna Times Industries Ltd until 1990. In 1990, co entered into a JV with Titan which ended in 2000. Co got listed in 1994. Timex Group is America’s Oldest Watchmaker, established in 1854 in Connecticut[1][2]

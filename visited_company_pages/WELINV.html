About

Incorporated in 2008, Welspun Investments and Commercials Ltd deals in shares, securities, textile products and commodities[1]

Key Points

Business Overview:[1][2]
WICL is a Core Investment Company which is categorized as a non-deposit taking Core Investment Company and is not required to be registered with Reserve Bank of India. It focuses on trading opportunities available in diverse sectors by leveraging the position of Welspun Group. Company is also engaged in investment segment.Company holds equity shares in Welspun Group companies which are engaged in businesses of Line Pipes, Steel, Infrastructure and Oil & Gas. Company’s revenue depends on the dividend declared and change in the stock market prices of the investee companies

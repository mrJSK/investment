About

Incorporated in 2012, Tara Chand Infralogistic Solutions Ltd provides cargo handling and logistic services[1]

Key Points

Business Segments
1. Warehousing & Multi-modal Transportation[1]
TCISL offers warehousing and logistics solutions, especially for steel warehousing and transportation services. Co. has handled 1.74 Mn MT in Q2FY25.[2]
- Key Services: The company supports multimodal transportation, managing logistics to ensure secure and timely delivery. They also handle consignment, stocking, and distribution of various products, especially steel, across different regions.

  1. Construction Equipment Rental[1]
    TCISL’s equipment rental segment supplies heavy machinery, including cranes, piling rigs, and aerial platforms, to the construction and infrastructure sectors. The rental fleet is extensive, featuring over 300 units, from large cranes with capacities up to 800 MT to aerial platforms reaching heights of 68 meters.
  2. Key Services: This segment caters to diverse project needs, including urban infrastructure, railways, cement, oil and gas, renewable energy, and road and highway construction. TCISL also provides specialized cranes and equipment to assist in critical tasks at client sites, enhancing project execution.

  3. Notable Projects: The segment has supported key infrastructure projects, including the Mumbai-Ahmedabad Bullet Train project and steel logistics solutions at facilities like RINL and SAIL. Its equipment rental services have also enabled TCISL to execute high-value contracts with companies like Larsen & Toubro and Tata Steel.

  4. Steel Processing & Distribution[1]
    TCISL also offers steel processing services as a horizontal integration of its steel logistics solutions. This involves processing TMT rebars and other steel products required in construction, providing a one-stop solution for clients.

  5. Key Services: The company performs processing on-site, allowing clients to source processed materials directly at their job sites, which enhances efficiency and reduces transportation requirements.

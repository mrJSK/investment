About

Nuvoco Vista Corporation Ltd (NVCL), is one of the largest cement companies and concrete manufacturers in India with a consolidated capacity of 25 MMTPA. It offers a diversified range of products such as cement, Ready-mix Concrete (RMX), and modern building materials i.e. adhesives, wall putty, dry plaster, cover blocks, and more.[1]

Key Points

Part of Nirma Group
NVCL is a part of The Nirma Group, which is a diversified conglomerate that manufactures products ranging from chemicals to detergents, soaps, healthcare products, and real estate development. The Nirma Group forayed into the cement business in 2014 through a greenfield cement plant in Nimbol. [1]
